Editorial Webinar

Tradeshows and other live in-person events have traditionally been very good channels for engaging and educating potential buyers. But with both marketing budgets and travel budgets cut, you need to find less labor intensive and more cost-effective ways of reaching large audiences. Webinars allow you to do just that, and their skyrocketing popularity in recent years means that they work.

Producing webinars that deliver against goals while providing for an engaging experience for busy professionals however, is a real challenge. That’s why our turnkey, end-to-end event production and promotion approach is ideal. We source speakers from our network of technology experts, we leverage our expertise in social media, email and other online marketing channels to engage an interested audience.
